About Us

LCDC Mission

The Little Children's Developmental Center is dedicated to providing safe, secure, stimulating, and age-appropriate developmental activities. A nurturing environment for low-income and working families with children between the ages of two and five. We view every child in our program as an extension of the community.

Philosophy

We believe that each child deserves an opportunity for full development. Every child needs to achieve self-knowledge, self-respect and to be equipped to reach his/her full potential. We strive to create an educational environment that includes: thinking and reasoning skills, fine/gross motor skills, positive and meaningful social interaction...

History

Little Children's Developmental Center ("LCDC") was founded in 1982 in San Francisco's economically disadvantaged Western Addition Community. The LCDC Program was unique because it provided high quality pre-school care, proper nutrition, social services, and parent training in one place, under one roof. In 1994, LCDC became a non-profit agency.
