Admissions Information

LCDC Parent Partnership

The Little Children's Developmental Center (LCDC) is a Parent Partnership School. This means that parents will be expected to participate in their children's development, growth, and education, in various ways (i.e. meetings, fundraisers, volunteering time, school or field trips, and donations).

Application Process

LCDC requires a completed application packet, a $50 non-refundable application fee, and a personal tour with the parent and child. The tour will be conducted by the center Director.

Priority will be given to siblings of families previously enrolled. Priority for reduced fee tuition shall be given to children residing in the neighborhood of the school. Children must be 2 years to 5 years of age.

Tuition

$1,600 / month

No credit is given for holidays, absences due to illness, vacations, or other leave. Reduced tuition plans may be available to families demonstrating economic need.

Hours & Schedule

7:00 AM to 6:00 PM Monday through Friday.

Children will not be accepted in the center after 9:00 AM unless the child is late because of an emergency or medical appointment.
