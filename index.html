Welcome to LCDC!

Little Children's Developmental Center, LCDC, is focused on making our children and community a success by providing safe, secure, stimulating, and age-appropriate developmental activities. Our programs promote self-esteem and encourage children to work and play together. They cover a wide range of activities, including art projects, learning games, and storytelling.

We provide:

  • Exceptional care
  • Safe and secure environment
  • Nutritious meals three times a day
  • Field trips
  • TANDEM Partners in Reading
  • Parent support program
  • Age appropriate and developmental practices

We also provide parent education programs, resources and referral services.

Special Programs

Community Engagement

In collaboration with San Francisco Safe Start, children meet with police officers who talk with the children, read to them, and give them a better insight into the community in which they live.

Early Literacy

To better support early literacy, we also offer the TANDEM Partners in Early Learning program. This is a take-home book bag program overseen by the Center.

Health Services

In association with the San Francisco Public Health Service, we also offer onsite dental, health and vision screenings with a certified Public Health nurse.

Mental Health

We also partner with Children's Council of San Francisco Mental Health Services which provides mental health consultations, assessments, and on site visits.
